Welcome to St. Mary's

St. Mary's Higher Secondary School Mawpdang has a very long history about its establishment. Nobody knows the exact date and the year. The history goes back to the arrival of the first Christian missionaries from Europe and it is presumed that the School might have started sometimes back in 2014.

The Lower Primary section was started by Fr. Constantine Vendrame, widely known as the Apostle of Khasis, in 1930. The school has grown into a heritage and an asset to the locality in education and culture.
