Mission Statement

St. Mary's Higher Secondary School Mawpdang envisages in providing quality education to the young pupils emphasizing self-realization, social efficiency, good citizenship, cultural transmission and regeneration and pursuit of truth based on gospel values.

Vision Statement

The school envisions sound and healthy education by cultivating habits of piety, virtue, self-reliance and discipline according to the educational system of Don Bosco.
